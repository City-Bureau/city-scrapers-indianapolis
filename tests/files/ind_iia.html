July 21, 2023

The Board of the Indianapolis Airport Authority will meet at 8:00 a.m. on Friday, July 21, 2023. Board members will either attend in person in the Airport’s Board Room (Room 11T.413) located inside the main terminal building of the Indianapolis International Airport, 7800 Col. H. Weir Cook Memorial Drive, Indianapolis, IN 46241 or instead attend remotely. The public is also invited to attend remotely. Immediately following the regular board meeting, the Reliever Airport Committee of the Board will meet briefly, followed by a tour of two reliever airports owned and operated by the IAA: Hendricks County Airport and Eagle Creek Airpark.

June 8, 2023


The public hearing upon proposed issuance of Bond Bank Bonds and Indianapolis Airport Authority Bonds will be held by the Authority on behalf of itself and the Bond Bank at 10:00 a.m. (Indianapolis time) on June 8, 2023, in the Authority’s Room 11T.413 located at the Airport terminal, 7800 Colonel H. Weir Cook Memorial Drive, Indianapolis, Indiana, at which time and place testimony, evidence or arguments for or against such ordinance from interested persons will be heard under rules established by the Authority. The public hearing is required by Section 147(f) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended. The public is invited to attend and comment on any of the matters herein noted.

Date & Time: Thursday, June 8, 2023 at 10:00 a.m.
